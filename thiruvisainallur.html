About

Location:Thiruvisainallur Temple Rd, Thiruvisanallurpandarvadai, Tamil Nadu 612105

Main god:Lord Shiva

The temple is open from 9.00 a.m. to 1.30 p.m. and from 4.00 p.m. to 7.00 p.m.

Significance:

Its is a syambu lingam

On chitirai 1,2,3 the sunrise fall on the lingam, the sundial in the southern temple wall is constructed such a way to measure the route of sun from sunrise to sunset

Here the nandhi safeguards as rishaba vahanam

This place alleviates the doshaas of people belonging to rishaba zodiac sign
