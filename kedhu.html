Kedhu Temple

Phto add paniru da roshan

About:

Location: Kezhaperumpallam, Near Kumbakonam (Kethu), Vanagiri, Tamil Nadu 609105

Main god:Naganatha Swamy(Shiva) Sowndara Nayagi(Parvathi)

Significance:

Poojas are performed to kethu at ragu kaalas and yamakanda kaalas to get rid of the bad effects of the planets
