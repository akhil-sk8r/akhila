About

Location: Kovil street, Thirumeyachur, Tamil Nadu 609405

Main god: Lord Shiva

The temple is open from 7.00 a.m. to 12.30 p.m. and from 4.30 p.m. to 8.30 p.m.

Significance:

Padalpetrasthalam

Those chanting lalitha sahasranama in this shrine are destowed with all grace and prosperity in life.

Mrutyunjaya Homa in this temple is performed for longevity
